About us

ICT Academy of Kerala is a Social Enterprise created in a Public Private Partnership model (PPP) for imparting ICT skills in youths of Kerala and to improve their skills in employment Industry. The Company is supported by Govt. of India, partnered by Govt. of Kerala and the ICT industry. The core objective of ICT Academy of Kerala is to improve employability skills amongst students who enrol in Technical, Technology and Arts & Science Institutions across the state.
